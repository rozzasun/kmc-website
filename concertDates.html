Concerts in 2022

We have tentatively booked three concert dates with hopes of holding live concerts for interested students in February and March. The dates are:

  • Sunday, February 20th from 2-5 p.m
  • Sunday March 13th from 2-5 p.m
  • Sunday March 27th from 2-5 p.m
Dates will be confirmed as we get closer to 2022. We will receive full details regarding procedure in January, but at this point we know that anyone entering the building will need to have a completed vaccination status with 14+ days post 2nd dose.

We plan to have shorter sessions with fewer performers at each concert. We will not be asking for membership fees or entry fees for these concerts and there will be no scholarship program or teacher marking involved.
