Overview

Hello Kanata Music Club Teachers, Members and prospective Members!

The Kanata Music Club will host seven concerts in the 2024/25 season. Venues are the Woodroffe United Church, 207 Woodroffe Avenue and the Kanata United Church, 33 Leacock Drive.

Concerts in 2024/25

We have booked seven Kanata Music Club concert dates. The dates and locations are:

  • November 17, 2024 - Woodroffe United Church. 4-6 pm (deadline Oct. 27)
  • Jan 12, 2025 - Woodroffe United Church. 2-4 pm (deadline Dec. 15, 2024)
  • Feb. 2, 2025 - Woodroffe United Church. 2-4 pm (deadline Jan. 12)
  • March 9, 2025 - Woodroffe United Church. 2-4 pm (deadline Feb. 16)
  • March 30, 2025 - Woodroffe United Church. 2-4 pm (deadline March 9)
  • June 1, 2025 -* Kanata United Church. 2-5 pm. (deadline May 11)
