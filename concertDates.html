Overview

Hello Kanata Music Club Teachers and Members and prospective Members!

The Kanata Music Club will host six concerts in the 2023/24 season. Venues are the Kanata United Church, 33 Leacock Drive, and the Woodroffe United Church, 207 Woodroffe Ave.

Our primary mission is to provide performance opportunity for students. As in 2022/23, there will be no scholarship programme or awards gala at the end of the season. We hope to see many of you at our upcoming concerts!

Concerts in 2023/24

We have booked six Kanata Music Club concert dates. The dates and locations are:

  • December 10th 2023 - Kanata United Church. 2-5 pm. (deadline November 19th)
  • February 11th 2024 - Kanata United Church. 2-5 pm. (deadline January 21st)
  • March 3rd 2024 - Woodroffe United Church. 2-5 pm. (deadline February 11th)
  • March 24th 2024 - Woodroffe United Church. Times TBA (deadline March 3rd)
  • April 7th 2024 - Woodroffe United Church. 2-5 pm. (deadline March 17th)
  • May 26th 2024 - Kanata United Church. 2-5 pm. (deadline May 5th)
