Concert Etiquette

  • All performers should arrive at least 5-10 minutes before the concert time, but not earlier than 15 minutes before concert time, to ensure a teacher supervisor is in house.
  • Members must plan to stay for the entire concert that they are performing in, showing support and interest in their fellow club members.
  • No rehearsals are allowed before the concert begins. String and woodwind players may use the piano for tuning purposes before the concert begins.
  • Members should dress appropriately for public performance. No jeans, hoodies or T-shirts. No winter boots on stage.
  • In the case when someone must enter or exit the hall during a concert, please do so very discreetly between performances.
  • You may record your own student/child's performance. Do not record any other performers without their parent’s consent. Recording equipment should be set up discreetly before the start of the concert.
