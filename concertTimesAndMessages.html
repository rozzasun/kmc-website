COVID-19 Notice Message

Due to the Covid-19 pandemic, The Kanata Music Club will not be resuming concerts until such a time that it is safe to do so for all involved. This decision involved the cancellation of our March 15th, March 31st and May 10th concerts as well as our Awards Concert on May 31st.

Because of these cancellations, it was decided that scholarships for the 2019/2020 season could not be fairly awarded and that funds would be held in reserve for the next season of the Kanata Music Club.

Decisions regarding the possibility of a concert season in the Fall of 2020 will be posted in August. Until that time, we hope members stay safe and continue making wonderful music at home! We all look forward to such a time when we can again come together to celebrate your musical accomplishments.
