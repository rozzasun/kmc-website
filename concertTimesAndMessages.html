Please note

  • All entering the church will need to be fully vaccinated. Please bring a printed proof of vaccination for students as well as the attending adult (who should also have a photo ID) - we will be checking vaccination status at the door

Registration

To register your students, teachers please use the Concert Request Form. We hope to be able to accommodate the student time constraints as listed on the Concert Request Form. Teachers and students are requested not to ask for specific times in the afternoon.
